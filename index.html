Server Rules

Get to know the rules of the server to help keep things running smooth.

1. Towing Rules

  • Winches are for recovery only; long-distance towing requires straps or a tow bar.
  • Roadtrains are allowed via mods.

2. Police Vehicles

  • BBPD-only. Whitelist required via Discord
  • No impersonation of police officers or vehicles

3. No Cheats/Exploits

  • No glitches, duping, or cheating of any kind

4. Roleplay & Driving Standards

4.1 Roleplay Encouragement

  • Use player-run services (towing/refuel) before roadside assistance if available

4.2 Speed Limits

  • Abide by town and city speed limits to avoid unnecessary incidents

4.3 Driving Standards

  • Drifting - Keep it to a track as much as possible
  • Street drifting is permitted but not on main roads where it may cause incidents
  • Racing - Keep races to a track or non-busy areas

5. Respect Players, Staff & The Server

  • NO Toxicity - Be respectful to all community members
  • Don't cause unnecessary stress to the server on purpose - (Excessive Cones, Furniture, loose cargo etc.)

6. Vehicle Limits

  • Max 6 spawns
  • Despawn idle vehicles & trailers - do not cause obstructions
  • Roadtrains are allowed via mods. Please create roadtrains away from towns/busy roads and do not cause obstructions for other players.

7. Cargo & Economy Fairness

  • Resupply depots after use - no server hopping for high-value cargo
  • Overloading is allowed. make sure it's all strapped and doesn't affect other players (wide loads etc.) Staff get final say so if you are told to unload, Listen.
  • Before overloading, inform PD so they can join in with any road closures etc.

8. Renting Land

  • Rented land must allow vehicle spawning at minimum
  • 1 Plot of land per player, no alt accounts to claim more land is permitted

9. Police Baiting

  • No intentionally provoking BBPD - chases should be organic
  • Compliance when pulled over = warning; fleeing = fines/PIT maneuvers/vehicle despawn if dangerous
  • If a major incident is caused between you and a player during a chase, end the chase, check in with the player and then continue if the BBPD officer is willing.

10. Liveries

  • No offensive or suggestive liveries (hate, slurs, NSFW)
  • Police liveries allowed on civilian vehicles only if make/model doesn't match official PD fleet
  • Imitation of PD vehicles is strictly prohibited

These rules are enforced by server staff. Violations may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community.
